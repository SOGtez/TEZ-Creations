About

what this place is

TEZ Creations is a personal lab where random creations get dropped for anyone to use — free. Tools, overlays, and little experiments, built whenever a good idea (or a bit of inspiration) shows up.

Most drops are stream tools for OBS — live score bugs, sub-goal trackers, GPS map overlays, gauntlet trackers. Others are standalone web apps that run entirely in your browser. New stuff lands in the Drops feed as it's made.
