How to get your data ▸
  1. Open Instagram's Export your information page (Settings → Accounts Center → Your information and permissions → Export your information).
  2. Choose your account, pick Some of your information, and select Followers and following.
  3. Set format to JSON and date range to All time, then request the download.
  4. Instagram emails a link (minutes, sometimes up to a day). Unzip it and find followers_1.json and following.json under connections/followers_and_following/.
  5. Drop both files above. Everything is parsed here in your browser — it never leaves this page.
