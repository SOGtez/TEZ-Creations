#003Stream Tool

Live Map

A live GPS map overlay for IRL & travel streams. Your location glides across a clean dark map in OBS — with speed, heading, and where you are. Powered by your RealtimeIRL feed.

1
Connect your RealtimeIRL feed
Paste your RealtimeIRL pull key. That's the only thing this overlay needs.
How do I get my pull key?
  1. Install RealtimeIRL (free, on iOS & Android) and sign in.
  2. Start a session in the app so it begins sharing your GPS.
  3. Open your pull key in the app / on rtirl.com and copy it.
  4. Paste it above. (Keep this overlay link private — it reveals your live location.)
2
Make it yours
The preview is live — it tracks the demo feed (or your own, once a key is set).

Preview shows the card on a stream-sized canvas. In OBS it sits in the corner you picked.

3
Drop it into OBS
Add a Browser source with this URL.
Add your pull key above to generate your OBS link…
  1. OBS → Sources → + → Browser
  2. Paste the URL · Width 1920 · Height 1080 (the card positions itself)
  3. Keep "Shutdown source when not visible" off so it keeps tracking
Keep this URL private. It contains your pull key, which reveals your live location. Don't show it on stream, and use a stream delay so your exact real-time position isn't broadcast instantly. Turn on Privacy mode above to coarsen the map.
