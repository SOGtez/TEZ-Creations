Game search & art by RAWG.

Add it in OBS as a Browser Source (width ~390, height ~600, transparent).

Chat commands (broadcaster + mods): !win <game>   !lost <game>   !active <game>. Use a name fragment or the list number, e.g. !win elden or !active 3.

How it works: add your games, enter your Twitch channel, and hit Create board — you'll get an OBS browser source URL. Drop that into OBS as a Browser Source (~390×600, transparent). Then you or your mods cross games off live with !win, !lost, and !active in chat. Your progress is saved, so refreshing OBS never loses your place. No account or install needed.
