About

One person, building
things worth dropping.

TEZ Creations is a one-person studio that builds and drops random creations, tools, and experiments for anyone to use — stream overlays for OBS and small web apps for the browser.

Everything here is designed, coded, and shipped solo. Built in the open, shipped when they're ready — no filler, no roadmap theatre, just genuinely useful things released when they earn it.

What I make

Two things, done properly.

Overlays that run live in your stream, and small apps that run in your browser.

2 categories

Stream Tools

Live overlays for OBS — drop a browser source into your scene and it just runs, updating on-screen while you stream.

  • Score trackers — live game scores + clock on your stream
  • Sub-goal counters — auto-updating goals that celebrate and roll over
  • Gauntlets — win-to-advance trackers your mods control from chat
  • GPS maps — live location overlays for IRL and travel streams

Browser Apps

Small, self-contained web tools — open a page and use it. Much of it runs on-device, so your data stays yours.

  • Username finders — hunt rare, short handles and check availability live
  • Instagram unfollower checks — see who doesn't follow back, all in your browser
  • No installs — everything runs in a tab, nothing to download
  • Privacy-first — much of it never leaves your device

The approach

Built solo, in the open, for streamers and creators — the tools I'd want to use myself.
