#004 Stream Tool

Game Gauntlet

A win-to-advance game tracker for OBS. Build your game list — then you or your mods cross games off live with Twitch chat commands. Your progress is saved, so refreshing OBS never loses your place.

Add a game manually (no art)

Game search & art by RAWG.

Add it in OBS as a Browser Source (width ~390, height ~600, transparent).

Chat commands (broadcaster + mods): !win <game>   !lost <game>   !active <game>   !addgame <name>   !removegame <game>. Use a name fragment or the list number, e.g. !win elden or !active 3.

How it works: add your games, enter your Twitch channel, and hit Create board — you'll get an OBS browser source URL. Drop that into OBS as a Browser Source (~390×600, transparent). Then you or your mods cross games off live with !win, !lost, and !active in chat — or even edit the list on the fly with !addgame / !removegame. Your progress is saved, so refreshing OBS never loses your place. No account or install needed.
